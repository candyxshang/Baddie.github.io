What is Onigashima Project?

Onigashima Project is a travel agency which was founded in 2013 and based in Bandung, we provides services to explore the beauty in every corner of Japan. We provide services such as airline tickets, lodging, vehicles, tour guides and other services. Tourists can visit downtown Tokyo, thousands of temples in Kyoto, or feel the experience of climbing the mountains of Fuji.
